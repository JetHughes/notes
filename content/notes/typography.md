## which font to use?
- depends on context
- recoomentations
	- slides and posters sans serif
	- printed text serif
	- web sans serif
	- sans serif is better in lower resolutions
	- combinations: (header sans, text serif)
	- comic sans good for dyslexia
	- logos should catch the eye
